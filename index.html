About Me

Hey there! My name is Wes and I am a full-stack developer from Atlanta, Georgia! My focus is HTML, CSS and JavaScript. My specialties include Node.js, React.js, Express, PostgreSQL! I'd say my strengths lie with frontend development in particular! My favorite aspect of frontend is completing apps that have wonderful interfaces. In my free time, I write, record and produce music. I enjoy coffee and casual tabletop games as well.

I attended Thinkful in the year 2021, which produced most of my linked projects below. Throughout the program, I had the priviledge of speaking with industry-level professionals. My time with Thinkful also allowed me to garner a disiplined autonomy, leading me to nurture my self-motivation.

As a developer, I have a large interest in software and app development. That's where a lot of my energy goes. Yet, I have a side interest in Raspberry and Arduino systems! I'm currently learning how to interact with these systems, and what capabilities they have. It's extremely exciting, and it is a very rewarding way to utilize code. Another way I find myself learning and using code is within the Unity3D environment. Unity is something I've been dabbling with since 2015, yet it wasn't until this year that I truly found a path for myself. I hope to make some small, fun games that showcase my interactive code.
